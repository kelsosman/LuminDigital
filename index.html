Why I'm Here

Lumin isn't just a next job.
It's the right one.

I've spent years across both branch banking software — where I watched on-premise systems get migrated to the cloud and felt every bit of that complexity — and digital banking products built for the cloud. That experience across both worlds gives me a clear-eyed view of what legacy infrastructure costs in speed, flexibility, and user experience. It's also why I find Lumin's foundation so compelling: cloud-native from day one means the product team gets to spend its energy on what actually matters — building features that move the needle for financial institutions and their members.

I've worked with a lot of fintech platforms and third-party vendors over the years. I know what good integration architecture looks like, and I know what it feels like when it's missing. Lumin's approach to partnerships and integrations stands out to me because it's thoughtful — not bolted on.

And the culture piece isn't filler. When a former colleague I respect deeply tells me she's never worked somewhere like this — that's signal. The 98% great-place-to-work certification and 99% employee engagement aren't just numbers I found on your website. They're the reason this role is at the top of my list, not just on it.
